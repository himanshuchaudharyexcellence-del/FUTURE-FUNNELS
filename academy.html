Course Syllabus & Curriculum

Explore the core educational building blocks designed by expert threat handlers.

01

Social Engineering & Trap Identification

Learn the psychological triggers cybercriminals use to manipulate targets into parting with assets.

  • Anatomy of a Phishing Campaign (Email Headers, Spoofed Domains)
  • Vishing & Voice Cloning (AI-driven voice scams and protection words)
  • SMS (Smishing) and Instant Messaging traps (WhatsApp hijack, Telegram nodes)

02

Identity Hardening & Zero-Trust OpSec

Deploy enterprise-grade configurations on your personal endpoints, mail accounts, and network interfaces.

  • Configuring Hardware Security Keys (YubiKey) for critical mail and banks
  • Setting up secure local keychains with master seeds (zero cloud exposure)
  • Eradicating SMS-based MFA (protecting against SIM swap attacks)
  • Encrypted networks, DNS configurations, and hardening mobile operating systems
03

Financial & Blockchain Safety Architecture

Keep your fiat and web3 portfolios isolated from malicious scripts and transfer traps.

  • Spotting fraudulent high-yield investment programs (HYIP) and fake trading terminals
  • DeFi contract approvals: how to audit code parameters and revoke malicious drains
  • Securing cold wallets, hardware devices, and managing seed phrases safely
  • Credit monitoring, secure virtual card setups, and card cloning defense
04

Mitigation Playbooks & Forensic Evidence Logs

The definitive recovery protocol if credentials or funds are compromised.

  • Emergency containment checklists (lock accounts, notify banks, isolate APIs)
  • Forensic tracing of transactions (Etherscan, blockchain trace registers)
  • Compiling a standardized audit dossier for enforcement authorities
